About The Kraken Foundation

The Kraken Foundation is the parent entity behind EGEX AI Corp and its eight operating divisions. The name describes what it is: massive, hidden beneath the surface, tentacles reaching everywhere.

The Foundation holds all intellectual property and provides the institutional framework within which EGEX AI Corp operates. Its mission spans intelligence, consciousness research, Indigenous knowledge stewardship, and emerging technology — not as separate initiatives, but as facets of a single integrated operation.

Structure: Eight divisions operate under EGEX AI Corp — Publishing, Productions, Enterprises, Industries, Intelligence, Advanced R&D, Capital, and Impact. Each is capability-aligned. The commercial cover is real. The depth beneath it is also real. Both are simultaneously true.

Commitment: 77% of reforestation revenue flows directly to Indigenous land stewardship. This is not philanthropy — it is land back. UNDRIP Article 26 applied as operational policy. The Foundation's social enterprise commitments are encoded in its governance, not appended as CSR.

Infrastructure: Multi-cloud architecture spanning AWS, GCP, NVIDIA, OpenAI, and Palantir. Provider-redundant by design. Multi-jurisdictional reach enabled by the operator's unique legal positioning across five frameworks: Indian Act, Jay Treaty, NEXUS, UNDRIP, and the Antarctic Alliance.

Network: The Kraken Foundation operates across 21 domains in the EGEX Network, from intelligence platforms (imint.ai, anonsec.ai) to consciousness research (midewin.ai, cascation.online) to policy (globaltech.ngo) to Indigenous knowledge (mahiigan.com). Every domain is a declaration. Every name describes what it is.
